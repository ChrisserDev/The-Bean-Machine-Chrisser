About Us

We are passionate about the coffee bean quality so we source our beans from all over the world in order to give you the best out there! We believe that each cup of coffee tells a unique story. Our carefully selected range of coffee beans represents the diverse flavors and aromas that coffee can offer. From the high-altitude plantations of Ethiopia to the sunny hills of Colombia, we source beans with character, integrity, and a commitment to sustainability. Visit our specailty coffee shop at La Rambla 204, where we serve the best coffee in Barcelona.
